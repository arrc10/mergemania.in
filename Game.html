  • How to play?

    1.Slide numbered tiles (2 or 4) on a grid using keys (W:up, S:down, A:left, D:right).
    2.When two tiles with the same number touch, they POP! and become one bigger tile (2 + 2 = 4, 4 + 4 = 8).
    3.Keep merging tiles to reach 2048 and win!
    4.A new tile appears after each slide (if you merged something).
    5.Game over if no empty spaces are left and no more tiles can merge.
